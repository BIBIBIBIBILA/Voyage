USYD Tower Adventure — Quick README

How to Play

Goal Explore multiple maps (e.g., plane → customs → campus → market), collect items, unlock passages, and advance the story.

Controls Arrow keys or WASD to move. Bump into objectsNPCs to interact (bump-to-interact).

Doors & Keys Door dN requires key kN. Keys are consumed when used.

PortalsEntrances Move between maps via portalsentrances (UI icons will indicate them).

Items & Crafting Pick up various items; some can be combinedused at special spots (e.g., an altar). Watch in-game hints.

Stats & Growth The right HUD shows Knowledge  Energy  Social. Certain dialogues, books, and events modify these.

Progress Rules (examples)
• After first disembark (plane → customs), returning to the plane via “ua” requires ticket “tk”.
• At the airport, if Energy ≥ 5 and Social ≥ 10 you can obtain “se”; with “se” you may receive “k14”.

Newly added items
• k11 — unlocks specific doorsevents.
• ne (Smart Note) — provides knowledge boosts in context and may be used for crafting.

Tip Doorsitemsportals have matching UI icons. Read on-screen prompts and dialogue for requirements.
